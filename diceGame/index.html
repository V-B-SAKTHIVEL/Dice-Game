Dice Game

Help

  • Dice game is the game in which the winning is entirely based on probability.
  • In this game, player 1 (you) roll the dice by clicking the button 'Roll Dice'.
  • The score of the dice is updated to the current score box. Click 'Hold' to store the score. This will finish your turn, and it's the player 2's (other) turn
  • Forgetting to hold the dice score will not be added to your total score.
  • The player who scores more than 100 wins the game!
